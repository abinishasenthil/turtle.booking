About Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a popular tourist attraction in Sri Lanka. It is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to the conservation and protection of sea turtles. The hatchery is involved in the rescue and rehabilitation of injured turtles, and also in the conservation of turtle eggs and hatchlings.

Visitors to the hatchery can see the various species of sea turtles that are native to Sri Lanka, and learn about their behavior, habitat, and conservation efforts. There are also opportunities to participate in releasing baby turtles into the ocean, which is a truly unforgettable experience.

Things to Do at Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery:

  • Learn about the different species of sea turtles
  • See the turtles up close and personal
  • Participate in releasing baby turtles into the ocean
  • Take a guided tour of the hatchery
