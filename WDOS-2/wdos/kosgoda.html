Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Conservation

Conservation Efforts

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery has been established to protect and conserve sea turtles, which are becoming increasingly rare due to habitat destruction and egg poaching. At the hatchery, they collect sea turtle eggs that have been laid on the beach, and hatch them in a protected environment. Once hatched, the baby turtles are released into the ocean.

The hatchery also provides a safe place for injured adult sea turtles to recuperate before being released back into the wild.

At the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, dedicated workers are at the forefront of conserving and protecting sea turtles.

Hatchery Tour

Turtle Hatchery Tour

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, where you'll witness the fascinating world of sea turtles up close. Our Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery Tour takes you on an immersive experience that combines education, conservation, and the opportunity to interact with these majestic creatures.

The hatchery's architecture and design reflect a blend of natural elements and functional spaces, creating a tranquil and engaging atmosphere for visitors.

Baby Turtles

Release of Baby Turtles

One of the highlights of the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery tour is the release of baby turtles into the ocean. Visitors can watch as the hatchlings are placed on the sand and make their way to the water.

The hatchery staff ensure that the baby turtles are released at the right time of day and in the correct location to give them the best chance of survival.
