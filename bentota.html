Bentota Turtle Hatchery

Rescue and Rehabilitation

Rescuing Estranged Turtles

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery plays a crucial role in the rescue and rehabilitation of sea turtles, offering them a second chance at life. The dedicated staff and volunteers work tirelessly to provide immediate care and attention to injured, sick, or distressed turtles that are brought to the hatchery.

The hatchery maintains a well-equipped medical facility to ensure the turtles' well-being and aid in their recovery.

Education And Awareness

Spreading Awareness

Hatcheries play a vital role in educating visitors and local communities about sea turtle conservation. They provide information on the importance of protecting sea turtles and their habitats, as well as guidelines for responsible behavior around nesting sites and hatchlings.

By implementing these education and awareness strategies, we can foster a deeper understanding and appreciation for sea turtles, inspiring individuals to take action and contribute to their conservation.

Collaboration and Partnership

Collaborating and Partnership

Hatcheries often collaborate with local communities, government agencies, and conservation organizations to implement comprehensive sea turtle conservation strategies. This collective effort ensures a coordinated approach to sea turtle care and protection.

Local communities have a vested interest in protecting sea turtles and their habitats, as their livelihoods often depend on healthy ecosystems.
